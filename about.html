About Negosyo Plan

We provide modern downloadable business plan bundles and AI blueprint technology for OFWs and entrepreneurs seeking structure, confidence, and growth. Founded by entrepreneurs, for entrepreneurs.

Our Mission

Negosyo Plan exists to help Filipino entrepreneurs plan smarter with downloadable PDF tools, strategy templates, and AI-powered guidance. We combine practical business planning with warm, trustworthy design for modern businesses — whether you're an OFW saving to start your first venture, or a founder ready to scale.

Why Choose Negosyo Plan?

Ready-Made Blueprints

Professional PDF business plan templates crafted for real-world execution — no blank page syndrome.

AI-Powered Guidance

AI-powered tools generate custom blueprints that fit your industry, budget, and market.

Market Adaptable

Designed to work for entrepreneurs in any local or international market.

Dedicated Support

WhatsApp and email support available 24/7 to help you at every stage of your business journey.

Blueprint OS Methodology

01Diagnose

02Design
