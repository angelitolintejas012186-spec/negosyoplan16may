Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our PDF business plan bundles, AI generator, downloads, payments, and support. Can't find your answer? Contact us directly.

Products & Bundles

What is included in each bundle?
Starter Bundle — Business plan template, financial projection model, market analysis guide, and launch checklist.

Founder Bundle — Everything in Starter plus marketing plan, operational templates, and a pitch deck framework.

Complete Set Bundle — All Starter and Founder resources plus a scaling checklist and revenue model workbook.

Custom Bundle — A tailor-made planning toolkit created specifically for your business idea, city, and capital.
Are the business plans suitable for beginners?
Yes! Our Starter Bundle is specifically designed for first-time entrepreneurs. The templates use plain language, step-by-step instructions, and pre-filled examples so you can start planning even if you have no prior business experience.
What file formats are included?
All products are delivered as PDF files. Some bundles also include editable Word (.docx) and Excel (.xlsx) templates for financial projections and budgeting. You can view and edit them on any device including mobile phones.
Which markets are the plans designed for?
Our plans are built to adapt to any market or city. The Custom Bundle can be tailored to your business location, local customers, and market needs.

AI Blueprint Generator

What is the AI Blueprint Generator?
The AI Blueprint Generator uses smart AI guidance to analyze your business type, budget, market, and timeline. It creates a structured outline with executive summary, budget guidance, timeline, risk assessment, and action steps.
Is the AI Generator free to use?
Yes! The AI Blueprint Generator is free to use. You can generate as many blueprints as you need. For a more detailed, professionally formatted PDF business plan, we recommend purchasing one of our bundles from the shop.
Can I download the AI-generated blueprint?
Yes, after generating your blueprint you can click the Download Blueprint button to save it. Blueprints are also saved locally in your browser history for future reference.

Payment & Downloads

What payment methods are accepted?
We currently accept Stripe card payments plus Philippine e-wallets such as GCash, PayMaya, GoTyme, and GrabPay. Other local e-payment providers are also available.
How do I receive my PDF files after purchase?
After a successful payment, you will be redirected to a download page where you can download your PDF files immediately. A download link is also sent to the email address provided at checkout. Downloads are available for 7 days after purchase.
What currencies do you support?
We display prices in PHP (Philippine Peso), USD, EUR, GBP, AED, and NGN. Currency conversion is automatic based on your browser settings, and you can switch currencies using the selector on the shop or home page.
Is there a refund policy?
Due to the digital nature of our products, all sales are final once the files have been downloaded. If you experience technical issues with your download, please contact us within 48 hours and we will resolve it promptly.

Support & Custom Requests

How do I request a custom business blueprint?
You can request a custom blueprint from the homepage form or by contacting us on WhatsApp. Provide your business idea, location, capital, and email — we will send a tailored preview within 48 hours.
How quickly do you respond to support messages?
WhatsApp messages are typically answered within 1-2 hours. Email queries are responded to within 24 hours. Our team is available 7 days a week including weekends.
Do you offer group or bulk pricing?
Yes! We offer special pricing for organizations, schools, OFW groups, and bulk purchases of 5 or more bundles. Contact us via WhatsApp or email to get a customized quote.

Our support team is ready to help you choose the right plan and get started.
